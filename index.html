About Me

I'm a PhD student within the Cluster of Excellence Data-integrated Simulation Science in the group of Bernard Haasdonk at the university of Stuttgart.
I'm a mathematician working in machine learning and numerical mathematics, in particular using kernel methods.

What do I research? ?

Research
Kernel approximation

I investigate approximation properties of kernels, in particular for kernel interpolation.
